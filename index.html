How to open a Kraken login account?

For creating a Kraken account, the following are the guidelines you need to keep in mind and apply the given steps carefully:

  • Open the account registration form- https://www.kraken.com/sign-up
  • Now, feed these details- email address, username, and Kraken login password
  • Choose your country > Agree to the terms > Create account
  • Complete some more steps and you’re done setting up your Kraken account

How to access and use the Kraken cryptocurrency exchange?

To access your account and start trading on the Kraken exchange, here are the easy steps that you need to follow:

  • Open the sign-in page on your device- https://www.kraken.com/sign-in
  • Enter the Kraken account login username and password
  • Click on the “eye” icon to view if you’ve fed the correct password
  • Thereafter, you can click on the “Sign in” button to access your Kraken account
